1. The Salt Trap

70% of the sodium we eat comes from processed foods, not the salt shaker. High sodium causes high blood pressure.

Tip: Rinse canned vegetables to remove up to 40% of the sodium.

Source: CDC

2. Frozen vs. Fresh

Frozen vegetables are often more nutritious than fresh ones because they are frozen at peak ripeness. They are also cheaper and last longer.

Source: Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics
